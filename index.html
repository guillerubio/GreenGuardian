Green Guardian: An IoT Solution for Plant Care

Key Features

Welcome to the Green Guardian project, an innovative IoT device designed to revolutionize the way we care for potted plants. By integrating cutting-edge technology directly into the soil of your potted plants, Green Guardian ensures optimal growth conditions through precise monitoring and control. Our device is self-contained, easy to use, and provides a hands-free approach to plant care.

Energy Harvesting

Green Guardian is powered by advanced energy harvesting technology, which captures and utilizes ambient energy from the environment. This sustainable power source reduces the need for external electrical power, making our device eco-friendly and efficient.

Water Control System

Our sophisticated water control system automatically adjusts watering based on the soil's moisture levels. By delivering the exact amount of water your plants need, Green Guardian prevents overwatering and underwatering, promoting healthier plant growth.

Cloud Service Apps

Leverage our cloud-based applications to monitor and manage your Green Guardian devices remotely. Previous work in cloud services has enabled us to provide a seamless experience that allows you to track plant health, receive updates, and access historical data for all your potted plants.
